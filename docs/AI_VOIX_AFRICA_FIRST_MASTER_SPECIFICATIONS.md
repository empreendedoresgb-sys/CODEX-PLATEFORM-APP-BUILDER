# AI VOIX – AFRICA-FIRST, GLOBAL BY DESIGN

## OFFICIAL MASTER SPECIFICATIONS

### Vision
AI VOIX is a next-generation Voice AI platform focused on African native languages, cultural voice identity, and world-class voice cloning.

The platform is designed to become the global reference for:
- multilingual voice AI,
- African oral languages,
- emotional and cultural speech synthesis,
- and identity-first voice cloning.

---

# Core Strategic Pillars

## Africa-First Linguistic Reality
AI VOIX is designed for:
- oral-first communication,
- tonal languages,
- code-switching speech,
- cultural cadence,
- storytelling rhythm,
- ceremonial and social speech patterns.

Africa is treated as a primary AI design layer, not a localization layer.

# Identity-First Cloning (Beyond MiniMax)

## Principle
Separate:
- who is speaking
from:
- what language is spoken.

## Capabilities
One human voice can speak:
- Wolof
- Pulaar
- Mandinka
- Yoruba
- Lingala
- Kriol
- and thousands of other languages

while preserving:
- identity,
- tone,
- rhythm,
- emotional signature,
- vocal texture.

## Technical Requirements
- Language-independent speaker embeddings
- Unified phoneme abstraction
- Cross-language identity preservation
- Noise-robust voice encoding
- Real-time inference optimization

# Code-Switching Intelligence

## Africa-Critical Requirement
Code-switching is treated as a default communication mode.

## Requirements
- Mid-sentence language switching
- Tonal continuity
- Rhythm preservation
- Identity stability during switching

Examples:
- Kriol ↔ Portuguese ↔ Mandinka
- Wolof ↔ French ↔ Arabic
- Yoruba ↔ English ↔ Pidgin
